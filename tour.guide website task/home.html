The name means “Big Island.” It is 19 miles (30 km) long, 12 miles (19 km) wide, and has an area of about 120 square miles (310 square km). Formed by volcanoes, the island is forested and has numerous craters and crater lakes. HISTORICAl:It is thought that the first inhabitants of Nosy Be arrived from the East African region and the Austronesian islands around year 800. They lived by fishing, farming and hunting, in harmony with the island's natural environment. The society of these early inhabitants was organized in small groups, led by tribal chiefs.
